# Noodly
 Welcome to Noodly! Noodly is an app created for those who love eating ramen to create and share their favourite ramen dishes. User starts by logging in or signing up to use Noodly. They can proceed to the "Build Ramen" page to create a ramen dish! This is where the fun takes place. What will you call your ramen dish? What brand of ramen is your "go-to"? Do you like, "Is it Samyang 2X Spicy Hot Chicken Flavoured Ramen? Is it Chappagetti? Noodly will then give you a choice of ingredient suggestions to make that bowl of ramen a personal culinary experience.
